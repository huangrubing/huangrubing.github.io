Dr. Rubing Huang

IEEE/CCF Senior Member, Associate Professor, Faculty of Information Technology, Macau University of Science and Technology

Rubing received his M.Eng and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. After that, Rubing spent one year at the Monash University as an academic visitor advised by Dr. Xin Xia and Dr. Robert G. Merkel, and also spent three months at Swinburne University of Technology as an academic visitor advised by Prof. T. Y. Chen. Currently, he is an associate professor at the Faculty of Information Technology, Macau University of Science and Technology. Before joining MUST, I was the faculty member of Jiangsu University, China.

He has more than 50 publications in journals and proceedings, including in IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering (TSE), IEEE Transactions on Reliability (TRel), IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computational Intelligence (TETCI), Journal of Systems and Software (JSS), Information and Software Technology (IST), IET Software, The Computer Journal, International Journal of Software Engineering and Knowledge Engineering (IJSEKE), Information Sciences, Security and Communication Networks (SCN), ICSE, ISSRE, ICST, COMPSAC, QRS, SEKE, and SAC. He has served as the program committee member of ICST2020 (poster track), SEKE2014-2019, SAC2017-2021, CTA2017-2019, and AI Testing 2019-2020, and has served as the reviewer for many journals such as TSE, TC, TRel, STVR, JSS, IST, SPE, IET Software, IJSEKE, TETCI, FCS, CCPE, NCAA, ESWA, etc. He is a senior member of the IEEE and the China Computer Federation, and a member of the ACM.

His current research interests include: 1) AI for Software Engineering, i.e., adopting AI knowledge and techniques to support the development of software engineering (including software requirements, testing, debugging, and maintenance); 2) Software Engineering for AI, i.e., applying the principles of software engineering to AI systems (such as AI systems testing and debugging); and 3) Blockchain Software Analysis and Testing.

I am looking for highly motivated Phd/MPhil students with strong background in Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, and Programming. Undergraduate students who are interested in my research are also warmly welcome to join us as research assistant. Please feel free to contact me via emails on the page bottom.
